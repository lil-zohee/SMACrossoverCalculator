How Does It Work?

The SMA Crossover technique uses the simple moving averages of a short term period and a long term period.  The long term period is generally x3 longer than the short term period.  For example if the short term sma is calculated over a 5 day period, then the long term sma will be calculated over a 15 day period.  Using these SMAs, whenever the short term sma crosses and goes above the long term sma, it indicates a buy signal.  Similarly, when the short term sma crosses and goes below the long term sma, it indicates a sell signal.
